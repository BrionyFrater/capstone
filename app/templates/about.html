About Us

Hi We are Gesture Creators ©

We're a team passionate about advancing communication through innovative technology. Our journey started with a simple idea: to connect sign language with spoken language, making communication more accessible for all.

As part of our capstone project, we are proud to introduce SignSync, an AI-powered Jamaican Sign Language (JSL) Translator. This tool enables seamless interaction between signers and non-signers, regardless of their proficiency in JSL. Collaboration is central to our work; we have consulted with organizations like the Jamaica Association for the Deaf to ensure that SignSync accurately interprets JSL.

Together, let's create a world where communication knows no limits.
