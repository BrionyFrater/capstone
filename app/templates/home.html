The Challenge

Why we made SignSync

With no reliable JSL translation tool available, communication barriers persist between deaf and hearing individuals. Existing technologies lack the capability to accurately translate Jamaican Sign Language into English—until now.

SignSync uses Artificial Intelligence to accurately interpret JSL in real time by capturing live sign language gestures through your camera and displaying the English translation instantly. SignSync also interprets English text into JSL gestures, based on a comprehensive database.

Key Features

Real-time Translation

Capture sign language gestures live and display English translations immediately.

Understands JSL's Complexity

Our AI understands the importance of facial expressions, eyebrow movements, and shoulder gestures, ensuring accurate interpretation.

JSL to Text

Uses AI model to instantly translate Jamaican Sign Language gestures into text with accuracy and real-time responsiveness.

Text to JSL

Effortlessly convert text input into Jamaican Sign Language, sourced from our comprehensive database.
